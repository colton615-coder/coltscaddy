# COLTSCADDY

Display name: "Colt's Caddy"

Vision: An AI caddie with a real personality that talks you through your round, learns your game, and coaches you between rounds.

## Three Pillars

- On-course assist
- Knows YOUR game
- Coach brain

## v1 SCOPE (locked)

A single-hole flow. User inputs shot situation (distance to target, lie, wind). App returns a caddie-style text recommendation with personality.

NO GPS, NO shot tracking, NO persistence, NO voice, NO accounts.

## NOT IN v1

- GPS
- Shot tracking
- Persistence
- Voice
- Accounts
